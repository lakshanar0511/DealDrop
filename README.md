# 🛍️ DealDrop AI – Hyperlocal Flash Sale Platform

## 📌 Problem Statement
Local retailers often struggle to sell overstocked or near-expiry products due to limited digital visibility. At the same time, nearby customers are unaware of these time-sensitive deals, leading to missed savings opportunities. This gap results in wasted inventory, reduced profits, and inefficient local commerce.

---

## 💡 Our Solution
DealDrop AI is a smart hyperlocal platform that bridges the gap between retailers and customers using real-time location-based deal discovery and an innovative reverse bargaining system.

The platform enables retailers to quickly list flash deals and allows customers to discover nearby offers and negotiate prices dynamically.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📍 Live Deal Radar Map
- Displays nearby deals in real-time
- Location-based discovery using map interface
- Color indicators for deal urgency (expiring soon, new deals)
- Distance and navigation support

### 💬 Reverse Bargaining System
- Customers can place custom price offers
- Retailers can accept, reject, or counter offers
- Real-time negotiation interface

### 👥 User Roles
- **Customers**: Discover deals, place offers, track expiry
- **Retailers**: Add deals, manage inventory, respond to offers

### ⏳ Flash Deal System
- Time-limited deals with expiry countdown
- Encourages quick purchases and reduces waste

---



## 🌱 Impact
- 📉 Reduces product wastage (especially food & perishables)
- 📈 Increases revenue for local retailers
- 💰 Helps customers save money
- 🏪 Promotes local businesses and hyperlocal economy

---

## ⚙️ How It Works
1. Retailers list products with price, location, and expiry time  
2. Customers view nearby deals on a live map  
3. Customers can directly purchase or place a price offer  
4. Retailers respond to offers in real-time  
5. Deals expire automatically after the set time
